Album 1: Up All Night

Released on November 18, 2011, in Ireland and the United Kingdom, the One Direction debut album was an absolute hit. In 2012, the album had a worldwide release by Syco Records. Up All Night mainly consists of faster, pop songs. The lyrics of most of the album songs include details on youth, relationships (including breakups), and empowerment. By the end of 2012 (after one year of release), the album became the third global best-selling album when they sold over 4.5 million copies worldwide, according to International Federation of the Phonographic Industry, becoming UK's fastest selling debut album of 2011. In the US, they sold over 175,000 copies in just the first week of release. Countless of the band's songs were performed shows and ceremonies (ex: 2012 MTV Video Music Awards), including the hit songs: What Makes You Beautiful, Gotta Be You, and One Thing.
