ONE DIRECTION

One Direction is a teen pop band. The five members were:

  • Harry Styles (leftmost)
  • Niall Horan (second from left)
  • Zayn Malik (middle)
  • Liam Payne (second from right)
  • Louis Tomlinson (rightmost)

The band first came together in 2010 but sadly broke up in 2016. Although it has disappointed millions of fans across the world, their music still lives on within the fans.

Formation of the Band

The band formed when each of the 5 singers auditioned as a singer for a British singing competition called "The X Factor" in 2010. However, when none of them were selected for the role, the judges grouped them together into the band known today as "One Direction."
