ONE DIRECTION

This is a website about One Direction, the pop band. The five members were Harry Styles (leftmost), Niall Horan (second from left), Zayn Malik (middle), Liam Payne (second from right), and Louis Tomlinson (rightmost). The band first came together in 2010 but sadly broke up in 2016. Although it has disappointed millions of fans across the world, their music still lives on within the fans.
